Feature Overview: The Inventory page provides a real-time view of all product stock levels with summary stats (Total, In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock). Each product row has an editable number input. When the value is changed, a "Save" button appears inline — clicking it calls supabase.from("products").update({ stock_quantity }) and immediately reflects the new count in the "Current Stock" column.

Auto-decrement: Stock is also automatically decremented when a customer completes a purchase (Stripe webhook handler uses Math.max(0, stock - qty) to prevent negative stock).
